BIO

DJ Phenom began cultivating his passion for music at a young age. Growing up in New York allowed him access to a city steeped in culture, diversity, and talent. He emerged on the city scene in 2005, quickly earning a repution for producing high energy that keeps the crowd hyped. His ability to read a crowd--and deliver--has landed him former residencies at NYC's Avalon (formerly Limelight) and Webster Hall, as well guest appearances in Las Vegas, Miami, and West Hollywood.

Sharing his craft with the world has always been DJ Phenom's main goal. He began working with Scratch DJ Academy in 2010, which led the way for several Celebrity Guest DJ opportunities on Royal Carribean's Oasis of the Seas.

DJ Phenom is more than an expert party rocker. His ability to seamlessly mix and manipulate music led to a former collaboration with Richmond, VA's HOT100.9FM. As the radio station's mix show DJ, Phenom crafted weekly mixes that showcased his talent, but also reflected his unique style that transcends across all genres.

In addition to effortlessly rocking the crowd, DJ Phenom plays an average of 30 corporate events per year for a diverse group of clients such as Macy's, Nordstrom, T-Mobile, H&M, and Victoria's Secret.

Currently, DJ Phenom still resides in the mid-Atlantic region, where he stays in high demand in Washington, D.C.; Virginia Beach; and Richmond. Along with spinning at the hottest and trendiest clubs, he has also succesfully forayed into the art of production and is presently working on original material. Check out Phenom, and see why the energy, versatility, and artistry he consistently brings to the turntables always leave the crowd wanting more.
